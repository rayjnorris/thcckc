Kicks 4 Kids KC – Update!

October 4, 2016
Kicks 4 Kids KC is sold out for this Thursday, October 6th!

If you would like to attend and have not purchased tickets, please contact Sara Adams at sadams@thcckc.org.  Sara will let you know if any new tickets are available or add you to a wait list.

This event helps fund the cost of quality pediatric primary medical, behavioral health and dental care for underserved and uninsured children in our community.

You can make a donation to Kicks 4 Kids even if you are unable to attend the event by visiting the Kicks 4 Kids registration page, and selecting the option: “I/we cannot attend the event, and would like to donate the amount below now by credit card or PayPal.” 

Turner House is grateful to our many returning and new sponsors this year.
