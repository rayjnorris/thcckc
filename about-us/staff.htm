Maria Reyes
Title: 
Healthy Steps Specialist

Maria Reyes is the Healthy Steps Specialist, providing developmental screenings for children ages 0 to 3.  Ms. Reyes also provides parent information and education to help their children develop language skills and reach age-appropriate milestones.  She has a Bachelor of Arts degree in Business Administration from the Technical Institute of Aguascalientes (Mexic0) and an A.A.S. degree in Child Growth and Development from Penn Valley College.  She is a Certified Bilingual Parent Educator for Parents as Teachers.  Prior to coming to Turner House in 2009, she was a Family Support Worker and Parent Educator for the Kansas City Missouri School District and Early Head Start of Shawnee Mission, and a Child Development Specialist volunteer with Heart of America Family Services.
